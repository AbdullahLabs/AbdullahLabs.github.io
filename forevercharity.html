Our Vision: A World Empowered by Perpetual Giving

Rooted in the timeless principles of Sadqa e Jaaria and charitable endowments, our mission is to create a sustainable model for giving. We believe that every act of kindness, no matter the size, has the potential to generate continuous blessings and positive change. By carefully stewarding your donations, we aim to build a perpetual engine of support for those in need.

Unlike traditional charities where donations are typically spent directly, we preserve the principal of your contribution. Instead, we leverage the returns generated from strategic investments to fund ongoing charitable activities. This ensures that your initial generosity transforms into a never-ending stream of support, embodying the true spirit of 'forever charity'.

By contributing to our initiative, you create a lasting legacy of compassion. Your impact transcends your lifetime, continuously benefiting those in need and bringing you the ongoing spiritual reward associated with Sadqa e Jaaria. You empower yourself and your loved ones to participate in a cycle of endless giving.

Your Gift, Perpetual Impact: Understanding the Process

You Donate

You make a contribution of any amount, knowing that every dollar has the potential to create lasting good.

Strategic Investment

Your donation is carefully invested in stable, income-generating assets such as dividend-paying stocks or real estate.

Generating Returns

The returns (dividends, rental income) from these investments are directed towards our charitable projects.

Perpetual Impact

The original principal remains untouched, ensuring a continuous flow of funds to support vital causes, forever.

We are committed to transparency, regularly reporting on the performance of our investments and the impact of our charitable activities. A portion of the growth of the principal is reinvested to expand our reach and impact over time.

Example of Enduring Impact

Your donation, no matter the size, contributes to a fund that generates perpetual support. For example, a one-time donation of $3,650, when invested at a hypothetical 10% return, could generate $1 every day, forever. That $1 could provide a meal for someone in need, every single day.

Even a smaller recurring donation, like $10/month, will grow over time and contribute to this perpetual fund, bringing us closer to our goal of providing daily sustenance for those who need it. Imagine the impact of increasing that to $20, $50, or more per month.

Your consistent support will help us build the principal faster, allowing the returns to make a difference sooner.

Our Story: Driven by a Vision of Enduring Charity

Founded on the principles of Sadqa e Jaaria, Forever Charity was established by a group of passionate individuals who believe in the power of sustainable giving.

We saw a need for a new model of charity that could provide continuous support for those in need, ensuring that the impact of a single donation would last for generations.
